Hog Wassup

Description

Hog Wassup is a story-oriented location based entertainment built in CAVE. In this game, the guest will play as a wizard with powerful magic, to go to the magic academy for the application of teacher. However, the magic academy is actually a scam, that the evil dean want to kill powerful wizard in this world. The guest will fill in a form to determine the accent for ice and fire spells, and use these two spells to defeat the monsters and boss.

Game Mechanism

The guests will say aloud the spells to release magic to defeat the monsters and boss, but he/she need to use the magic spell that has opposite attributes of the monsters to defeat them.

Platform

CAVE, Unity

CAVE is an immersive virtual reality environment where projectors are directed to three of the walls of a room-sized cube. In the CAVE environment, we also introduce the Motion Floor to simulate movements on bouncing terrain.
