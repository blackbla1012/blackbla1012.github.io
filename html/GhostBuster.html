Ghost Buster 2022

Description

Dream Guardian is a VR game that guests play as a Teddy Bear and use the magic flashlight and dream catcher to catch the nightmares to give back the little boy a good dream.

Game Mechanism

The nightmare enemies only could be seen where the light shines by the flashlight. The guest can wave the dream catcher to catch the nightmares near him/her, also can throw it to catch far ones. There're short range attacking nightmares together with long range attacking clouds. There'll be a boss fight session at the end of the game.

Platform

Unity, Oculus Quest 2 with pass through
